Welcome To Nadiad Balkan-Ji-Bari

Its founder president Freedom fighter late Shri Mohanlal Chhaganlal Shah administered Balkan-Ji-Bari and played an important role in the development of the organization till 1995. After that, Shri Dinsha Patel, Honorable Minister of Mines in Central Govt. of India, became the President of Nadiad Balkan-Ji-Bari till date. Under his able guidance, Balkan-Ji-Bari has increased its activities manifold. Nadiad Balkan-Ji-Bari is a Registered Charitable Organization and is recognized by the Government of India. It has also achieved the prestigious best activity for children welfare award from the "Gujarat State Social Welfare" department in the year 1992.
